My experience as a Waiter

Being a waiter as not as easy as one thinks. It requires great patience and memory and needs to have great flexebility as well as needs various skills like handeling many plates, drinkes, dishes, etc at a time. Some people may and nag on every little thing but one needs to be clam and respectful even in those situation.

Waiter can't be disrespectful to the customer as the resturant, hotel, resort, etc rating of services will degrade. But a waiter is a very importent profession on such establishment as the customer mostly interacts with only the waiter and the staff.But,not the chef since chef already have many things on their hands so the waiter is the face of the establishment.
